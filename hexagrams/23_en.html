Splitting Apart

剥 Bò - Splitting Apart

卦辞

剥。 It does not further one to go anywhere.

Story

You may find yourself in a situation of gradual but inexorable decay. Like a crumbling structure, the integrity of the whole is undermined as the darker elements ascend and erode the light. In times such as this, retreat and allow things that have run their course to end.

Symbolism

The Earth above the Mountain symbolizes a situation where an inferior force rises while the light decays. This portrays a state of significant deterioration where the integrity of the whole is threatened by the disintegration of vital elements.
